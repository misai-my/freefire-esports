OB53 — Undersea Mystery

The newest patch brings an ocean-themed event layer, a new rush-down character named Ray, big BR and CS utility updates, esports-ready Ban & Pick in CS custom rooms, and a broad weapon rebalance.

OB53 Highlights

Undersea Mystery

OB53 adds a full water-themed event layer to BR with the Undersea Realm, Fishing Ponds, Bubble Airdrops, and the new Hydro Blaster.

Ray — New Character

Ray’s Bond of Eclipse tags an enemy in a forward fan, can instantly knock them down once they hit low HP, then resets on a tagged takedown and restores HP.

Preset Flexibility

Players can now switch Presets and Loadouts during the prep phase, then choose from Preset 1–5 once the match starts.

CS Random Events

Clash Squad gets rotating special rules like melee starts, auto Gloo Walls every 5 seconds, or carrying over a previous round weapon for eliminated players.

Weapon Meta Shake-Up

AC80 is nerfed, SKS is buffed, M24 becomes upgradeable, sniper armor penetration rises, and limb-damage scaling is removed for more predictable fights.

Esports Custom Rooms

CS custom rooms now support an Esports preset with 11 rounds, Cyber Airdrops, and a dedicated Skill Ban & Pick phase.

Battle Royale

Undersea Mystery Event

  • Whale Plane replaces the normal starting plane with a themed whale model.
  • Undersea Realm appears through an entrance at Nurek Dam on Bermuda, with rich loot and a sword statue that gives high-tier rewards.
  • Hydro Zone replaces the High-tier Loot Zone during the event and lets players and bullets pass through the water wall.
  • Fishing Ponds offer loot and buff-granting fish that follow the player.
  • Bubble Airdrops can be captured faster by popping and charging nearby bubbles with teammates.
  • Hydro Blaster sprays water beams and can fast reload through a QTE.

Preset / Communication Updates

  • Players can switch Presets and Loadouts in the preparation phase.
  • After the match starts, players can choose among Preset 1–5 but cannot swap specific active or passive skills.
  • Quick Messages add Group Up, better enemy/location marking, and smart prompts for Arsenal, Airdrop, Revival Point, and landing weapon checks.

Other BR Adjustments

  • Most devices are now single-use, except Launch Pads, Portals, and Mini Turrets.
  • Mini Turret gets faster fire rate, longer duration, and longer range.
  • Tactical Market removes Hit List, Arsenal Key, and active skill cards, while adding Heal UAV-Lite.
  • Arsenal upgrade cost is reduced in solo and duo.
  • Out-of-zone and plane-cabin audio are optimized.

Clash Squad

Undersea Realm in CS

  • The Undersea Realm joins the Bermuda map pool.
  • Hydro Blasters are added to the CS Store.

Random Events

  • Start each match with 1000 CS Cash.
  • Receive Super Meds or Super Energizers at round start in some event variants.
  • Get a Gloo Wall every 5s.
  • Start with a Lv. 2 Helmet and Lv. 2 Vest.
  • Receive a random melee weapon at round start.
  • Eliminated players can respawn with the weapon they held at the end of the previous prep phase.

Official OB53 Clash Squad gameplay update art.

Tactical Market & Minimap Optimization

  • An in-match Tactical Market button lets players deploy default or last-used gadgets during battle.
  • Tactical Market can now be used while moving.
  • Gadget durations now start after the prep phase.
  • The Bermuda minimap is optimized for clearer visuals and better loadout deployment reads.
  • UAV-Lites are removed from the Tactical Market in CS and Jammer devices are added as anti-scan utility.

This is one of the most esports-relevant parts of the patch because it reduces free intel and adds stronger anti-scan counterplay.

Characters

Ray — The Watchman

Bond of Eclipse sends sunpower in a 30m fan-shaped range to tag the first undowned enemy hit for 10s. If Ray reduces the tagged enemy to 40 HP or below, they are instantly knocked down. If the tagged target is knocked down or eliminated while tagged, the skill resets and Ray restores 10 HP/s for 3s.

  • Cooldown: 45s
  • Tag visibility: link is visible within 40m to Ray and the tagged enemy
  • Best fit: rush-heavy streak comps

Skill Balance Notes

  • Buffed: Dasha, Shani, Jota
  • Nerfed intel: Koda, Moco, Otho, Shirou
  • Nerfed control/damage: Nero

Official OB53 character update art.

Balance Details

  • Dasha: Highlight mode duration increased from 6s → 10s.
  • Shani: Shield Point duration increased from 5s → 10s.
  • Jota: Any knockdown now triggers the 20% HP recovery, not just gun knockdowns.
  • Koda: Aurora Vision duration reduced from 10s → 8s.
  • Moco: mark duration reduced from 4s → 3s, moving mark extension from 5.5s → 4s.
  • Otho: teammate mark/slow duration reduced from 4s → 3s.
  • Shirou: attacker mark duration reduced from 10s → 8s.
  • Nero: dreamy-space damage reduced from 16 → 14 HP/s with faster post-space Gloo recovery for opponents.

Weapons & Balancing

Core Meta Changes

  • Limb damage multiplier removed; limb hits now deal body-shot damage and armor protects limbs.
  • M590 gains a muzzle slot; PLASMA and Kord gain foregrip and stock slots.
  • AC80 nerf: damage -6%, extra Shield Point damage 20% → 0%.
  • SKS buff: rate of fire +6%, range +15%, reload speed +10%, mag 16 → 10.
  • Shield Gun and Charge Buster receive major adjustments to improve viability.

Sniper Changes

  • M24 becomes upgradeable.
  • M24 base weapon: damage +60%, mag 15 → 5, headshot rate 5.5 → 2.5, no longer penetrates Gloo Walls.
  • AWM / AWM-Y / M82B / VSK94 / Kar98k: armor penetration +20%.

Official OB53 weapon update art.

Close-Range / Economy Adjustments

  • MAG-7: switch speed +15%, rate of fire +5%, movement speed +5%.
  • UMP: range +5% and gains a muzzle slot.
  • FAMAS in CS: 1800 → 1600 CS Cash.
  • M1014-III and Winchester-III added to Cyber Items for 1900 CS Cash.
  • M24-II and M1014-II added to BR vending at 600 FF Coins; VSS costs 100 FF Coins with no limit.
  • Shield Gun becomes ground loot in Solara.

For FF analysis, the standout swing is the AC80 nerf plus a broader sniper package that raises armor penetration and makes the M24 a real progression weapon.

System & Custom Rooms

Official OB53 Pyro login streak art.

Pyro Login Streak

A new daily login system adds Pyro, a profile/lobby companion that evolves through three stages and unlocks cosmetics through Pyro’s Vault.

HUD Sharing & Smart Adjust

Players can now share HUD layouts through social, friends, links, and QR codes. A new Smart Adjust feature suggests layout changes from usage data.

Ban & Pick in CS Custom Room

The new Esports preset enables 11 rounds, Cyber Airdrops, esports-aligned economy, and a dedicated Skill Ban & Pick phase with unique active skill picks.

Performance & Visuals

The patch adds another Experimental Frame Rate Boost option and new camera filters: Cyberpunk and Golden Era.

Social / Teaming

Booking is reworked so accepted booking requests immediately reserve a friend for the next lobby. Quick Messages also now support pre-match recent-highlight display and in-match speech-to-text for eligible players.

Speed Limit Adjustments

Stacked speed effects are toned down in CS, including smaller boosts from Team Booster and Super Bonfire, plus restrictions on overlapping Alok auras.

OB52 Highlights

Jujutsu Kaisen Collaboration

Collect Cursed Energy in BR to unlock iconic techniques and tools, plus themed areas and special interactions across modes.

Includes unlockable Jujutsu like Divergent Fist, Hollow Purple, Domain Expansions, and more (event rules apply).

New BR Location: Neon City

Enter via Rift Portals, explore a nighttime metropolis, then fast travel back out to different areas of the map.

New Character: Morse

Stealth Bytes enables stealth flanks and safer utility actions (revive, vending, vehicle moves) without being easily spotted.

BR: Breakable Arsenal + Ultra Airdrop

Smash into arsenals for high-value loot (but it triggers a reveal), and fight for a capture-based Ultra Airdrop in late game.

System: Camera + Highlight Photos + FPS

New camera tools, auto-captured highlight moments, and experimental frame rate boosts for smoother play.

Jujutsu Awakening (Event Summary)

Battle Royale

  • Cursed Energy piles across maps; collect by proximity, eliminations, or vending.
  • Spend energy to unlock Jujutsu and a Cursed Tool via a dedicated panel.
  • Key unlocks include Divergent Fist, Hollow Purple, Ember Insects, and Domain Expansions like Unlimited Void / Malevolent Shrine.
  • Revival Points are imbued with Cursed Energy for faster return-to-fight pace.

Treat this as a “hot drop” meta patch: expect volatile late-game swings when Jujutsu unlocks come online.

Clash Squad

  • Jujutsu techniques (incl. Hollow Purple, Divergent Fist, Ember Insects, and Domain Expansions) appear in CS Store and Cyber Airdrops.

Bermuda’s Peak is renovated into a themed “High School” area for the event — expect rerouted fights and new cover lines.

Characters

Morse — New Character

Stealth Bytes (Active) puts you into Stealth Mode for smart flanks and safer utility actions.

  • Hard to see beyond 16m, cannot be detected/scanned, and gains 20% movement speed.
  • Aim assist triggers if enemies are within 4m.
  • Duration up to 15s; cannot fire while stealthed; 1s exit delay.
  • Cooldown: 45s.

Caster angle: “Morse enables stealth timings—watch for late-round backstabs and stealth resets into revive/vending plays.”

Skill Balance (Quick Notes)

  • Tatsuya: cooldown reduced; reset now only if knockdown happens within 10s after activation.
  • Wukong: similar reset rule adjustment + cooldown reduction.
  • Xayne: stronger shields + longer duration for aggressive pushes.
  • Nero: “better control” (casting/handling optimization noted in official summary art).

Official character update art.

Weapons & Balancing

Core Changes

  • Weapon rarity colors added for quick tier readability.
  • Explosive weapons capacity reduced (FGL-24 / RGS-50: 3 → 2).
  • Upgradeable weapons: Kar98k and FAMAS upgrade mechanism removed (now match their “III” attributes in practice).

Airdrop Buffs

  • Groza: damage +3%, armor pen +10%, range +8%.
  • SVD: accuracy +20%.
  • M249: rate of fire +5%, armor pen +5%.
  • Thompson: rate of fire +5%.

Other Notables

  • MAC10 family: damage +8%, accuracy +15%.
  • VSS: armor pen +10%, accuracy +5%.
  • Heal Pistol: auto-target teammates; ROF +30% with adjusted heal economy (incl. Heal Pistol-Y hitting 2 allies).

Meta read: expect more airdrop priority again, plus stronger “support heal” consistency with auto-targeting.

OB51 Highlights

New Character: Nero

Nero brings a tactical buff that helps squads push or hold by converting Gloo usage into temporary combat advantages. Great for coordinated rushes and late-circle skirmishes.

Classic Winchester returns reworked for mid-range precision. Thompson-X is a controllable SMG built for aggressive entry fragging.

Loadout Rework

Team utilities like Team Booster, Enhance Hammer, Tactical Market, and Super Leg Pockets streamline BR setup with clearer choices and stronger identities.

BR Quality-of-Life

Waypoint pings, improved Weapon Rack info, and new utility items such as Gloo Mushroom and Gloo UAV-Lite make rotations and scouting more readable.

Clash Squad Tuning

Purchase flow refinements, Gloo and grenade adjustments, and pacing tweaks to reduce early snowball and encourage counterplay.

Solara Map Balance

Cover, loot, and landing adjustments across key POIs to increase fair fights and diversify rotations.

OB51 Characters

Nero — Featured Character

Nero’s skill rewards smart utility usage and team play. It triggers a short-window buff around Gloo interactions so your squad can breach, trade, and reset more safely. Best paired with entry SMGs/shotguns and a support running extra Gloo.

  • Synergizes with utility-heavy rotations and late-circle holds.
